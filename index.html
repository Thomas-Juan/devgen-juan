About the Lab

The Smith Lab studies microbial evolution and host–microbe interactions. We combine experimental evolution, genomics, and computational biology to understand how microbes adapt to changing environments.

Research

Microbial Evolution

Understanding how microbes adapt through mutations and natural selection.

Host–Microbe Interactions

Investigating how microbial communities interact with host organisms.

Genomics

Using sequencing technologies to explore microbial genetic diversity.
